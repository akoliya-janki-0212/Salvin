Salvin Ribbon Blender is one kind of mixer to mix different type of powders. Horizontal mixer is precisely designed to maintain combination of three major dimensions is Diameter, Ribbon width and Pitch. Powder is got Rotary as well as horizontal motion in positive direction to get effective blending which is happen by design perfect sizing of inner and outer ribbons.
